Hot Potatoes

HP Is an audio conversation tool that brings back the joy in online connection by making it fun, dynamic and constructive. Creating a neutral environment where your data and privacy is only yours. And where the knowledge that is exchanged is searchable and open for research and cross referencing.

Come Play! Gamelike interface

Hot Potatoes invites and gives flexibility to any facilitator to create their own dynamics. We admit to be inspired by sociocratic and methods like fishbowl, but even these are constantly evolving... See what works best for your session to reach conclusions to help solve the Topic Goals!

Try it!

Talk and Listen! Audio focussed

Hot Potatoes is Audio only, so you can really focus on the content instead of the Tshirt you are wearing, or the cat that passes in the background. Conversation becomes an interactive podcast experience, share your ideas while doing the dishes ;) We want to make it easy for yo to share those ideas.

Listen without distractions

Find all interactions in a Magic Time line

Comments and other interactions are linked to the timeline. Apart from easy reference, this means you can come back to the recorded session and continue the conversation. Construct a true knowledge base!

How does it work?

Rewards for Value by participating

Value is created by estimating correctly your own level of participation + the actual solving of goals + creating a high interest and contribution to the general knowledge base of a Topic. So if you participate like you staked and help solve the goal of a highly active (Hot) Topic you are creating value and thus gain rewards. This feature is being build but only active in Phase II.
If you have ideas about this, please drop us a line. Or start a topic ;)
